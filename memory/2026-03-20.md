### Issue: AI Close/Reopen Loop
- Symptom: AI closes ROBO/WLFI → scanner re-signals same symbol → bot reopens → AI closes again
- **Root cause:** `Tracker.remove_position()` wipes the position from tracker after AI close, so `_process_signals` sees no position and reopens it
- **Fix applied (in bot.py):**
  - Added `_ai_close_cooldown: dict[str, float]` and `_ai_cooldown_seconds = 600` to `__init__`
  - In `_process_signals`: after "already have position" check, skip if symbol is in cooldown dict
  - In `_execute_ai_close`: after successful close, set `self._ai_close_cooldown[symbol] = time.monotonic() + self._ai_cooldown_seconds`
