## Key Lesson
- **Slow pace kills.** ADHD brain craves rapid progress. When things move slow, feels like dying.
- **External deadlines work.** Accountability partner or public commitment > willpower alone.
- **Smaller than you think.** Projects are always 3x too big for one ADHD sprint. Cut to minimum viable, ship at 70%.
- Walking is the best ADHD medicine besides the actual medicine. Keep it.
