# Karpathy's LLM Knowledge Base: Build an AI Second Brain

Breakdown article from codersera.com explaining Andrej Karpathy's LLM wiki approach.

Published April 3, 2026. Describes the shift from using LLMs for code generation to building self-maintaining personal knowledge bases.

Key points covered:
- Three-layer architecture (raw/ → wiki/ → outputs/)
- Compilation step and linting passes
- Schema layer (AGENTS.md / CLAUDE.md)
- Why it skips RAG in favor of structured markdown
- ~100 articles, ~400K words on a single research topic without Karpathy writing any of it
