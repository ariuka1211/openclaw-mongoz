# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher. Coined the term "vibe coding." Originator of the LLM Wiki pattern.

## Role in Personal Knowledge Management

Published the original [[LLM Wiki Pattern]] gist on 2026-04-03 that sparked the second brain movement. Described using LLMs as a "self-maintaining personal wiki" — shifting from code generation to knowledge manipulation.

## Key Quote

> "Obsidian is the IDE; the LLM is the programmer; the wiki is the codebase."
