# Corey Ganim's Monetization Playbook

**Original:** [Tweet by Corey Ganim](https://x.com/coreyganim/status/2041234736954347935), 2026-04-06

## Summary

A productized service playbook for the LLM wiki pattern: build for yourself → sell setup ($1,500-3K) → monthly retainer ($300-500/mo) → bundle training (+$500). Targets agencies and consultants with scattered client data.

## Key Claims

1. Agencies/consultants have years of client data, research, proposals, call transcripts scattered everywhere
2. One client = $2K setup + $400/mo recurring
3. Ten clients = ~$57K in year one
